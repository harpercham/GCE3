Partners

Mekano4

Mekano4 specializes in supply of technical solutions for construction and maintenance of bridges and structures, also for geotechnical projects with their bars and ground anchorages. Majority of their involvement includes posttensioning, bridge bearings, expansion joints and construction methodologies for bridges.

Sanshin Corporation

Sanshin Corporation, one of our partner is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan operates as a construction contractor for civil engineering projects. Specializing in ground improvement works methods by soil mixing, jet grouting, ground permeation etc. Sanshin Corporation is also involved in other activities such as slope protection, ground anchoring, pile and foundation construction.

Halfen Moment

Halfen Moment is our main partner in the supply of posttensioning systems. In addition, they also supply products such as mechanical splices to reinforcement and construction accessories.




Subsidiaries

Urban Civil Engineering SDN BHD

Urban Civil Engineering SDN BHD is our subsidiary company stationed in Johor which excels in bored piling and micro piling. Additional involvements are post-tensioning, site investigation, reinforced concrete works and heavy lifting.

GCE (Structural) Pte Ltd

GCE (Structural) Pte Ltd is our subsidiary company providing technical support in post-tensioning works. Other involvements include structural strengthening/repair, structural health assessment and specialized works.
